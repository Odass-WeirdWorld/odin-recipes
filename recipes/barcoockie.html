Bar Coockie Recipe

“Amazing bar cookies with oatmeal, chocolate chips and caramel ice cream topping all layered to create THE BEST Bar cookie around. ”

just realized I've never rated this, although I've made at least 50 batches! These are so wonderful, I found them on this site after tasting the same recipe at a popular sandwich chain, called "Sheila's Dream Bars" there.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 ½ cups brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 ½ cups butter, melted
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar caramel ice cream topping
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
